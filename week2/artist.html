米山 舞（よねやま まい）

Mai Yoneyama (Japanese: 米山舞, born July 12, 1988, in Nagano, Japan) is a Japanese illustrator and animator known for her dynamic, colorful, and emotionally expressive art style. She began her career at the renowned animation studio GAINAX, where she worked as an animator before joining Studio TRIGGER, contributing to acclaimed projects such as Kill la Kill and Kiznaiver. After gaining recognition for her fluid animation and striking compositions, Yoneyama transitioned into freelance illustration, producing artwork for games, light novels, and music videos. Her distinctive use of light, color, and movement reflects her background in animation. She held her solo art exhibition and released an art book titled “EYE”, which highlights her unique vision and storytelling through illustration. Today, she is affiliated with SSS by applibot, continuing to influence both the anime and illustration industries with her futuristic, vibrant, and emotionally resonant style.
